Gather for orphans and widows

We come together to support orphans and widows. As a result of the war, thousands of children have been left without parents, and tens of thousands of families have lost their breadwinners. Today, we stand as their psychological and financial support system. Our volunteers provide emotional assistance to widows, while we gather food packages for children and women. ToGether, we can make a difference in their lives. Together, let's show them they are not alone
