About Gather

You contact us regarding the car
you would like to receive from our fund

In this difficult time, it is extremely important to unite for the sake of a common goal - OUR VICTORY! Each one of us tries to be useful for the Motherland and does everything in his, or her power! We are are no exception. We organized a volunteer fund, which includes a group of volunteers who already had experience in acquisition and transporting the autos for the needs of the Armed Forces of Ukraine!

In order to maintain the combat capability of units of the Armed Forces of Ukraine during active hostilities, there is a need for rapid transportation of ammunition, food, and personnel. Since the active phase of the war is currently being waged, there is a catastrophic lack of transport for the above-mentioned purposes, so there was a need for ordinary reliable machines with high off-road capability. Considering the relatively low cost of special military transport, it is possible to purchase more vehicles and provide them to a larger number of combat units.

What do we offer? The main goal of the fund is to deliver a car to units that are in dire need of transportation. Therefore, after talking with the military guys, we have contacts of people who are responsible for the units where these autos are required. How can you help us? If you have the opportunity, you can make your contributions.

We will also be grateful for the dissemination of information about our fund.

How do we work?

You contact us regarding the car you would like to receive from our fund

You take a place in a queue and once it is your turn, our team will contact you to open the fundraising. What we will need from you are just the following documents, on behalf of your military unit headquarters (A####) :

  • Official request for the car, so that the car is recognized as humanitarian aid at customs
  • Official request for the specific amount to be fundraised by our partners (upon request)
  • Gratitude document for our partners (upon request)
  • Act of acceptance \transfer of the car

You contact us regarding the car you would like to receive from our fund

We will find a suitable for you car that will meet all of your desired criteria, and will bring that car to you without any additional fundraising. Thus, this can be done without a queue. Such an algorithm will shorten the period of you receiving the vehicle. What we will need from you are just the following documents, on behalf of your military unit headquarters (A####) :

  • Official request for the car, so that the car is recognized as humanitarian aid at customs
  • Act of acceptance \transfer of the car
